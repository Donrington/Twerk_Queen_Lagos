FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with TwerkQueen Lagos?

+

Simply visit our “Register” page on the website. Fill out your details and wait for our confirmation. We’ll guide you every step of the way!

Why should I learn to Twerk?

+

Twerking is not only a fun dance style—it builds core strength, confidence, and fosters a vibrant community. Get fit, express yourself, and have a blast!

What is the fastest way to become a better twerker?

+

Practice consistently, watch tutorials, attend TwerkQueen workshops, and don’t be afraid to perform in front of a crowd. The more you dance, the quicker you level up!

Do I need a partner?

+

Absolutely not! Twerking is about individual expression. Whether you come solo or with friends, TwerkQueen Lagos is open to all.

What should I wear for classes?

+

Wear something comfortable and form-fitting. Leggings, shorts, or yoga pants are great. Make sure you can move freely and confidently!

Do I need special shoes?

+

Flat, flexible shoes or sneakers work best. High heels aren’t required unless you’re practicing a specific twerk style or performance look.

Can twerking help me reach fitness goals?

+

Definitely! Twerking engages core muscles, improves stamina, and offers a total-body cardio workout. You’ll see progress in no time.

What is your refund or cancellation policy?

+

We offer flexible class credits and partial refunds for monthly subscriptions. Check our “Terms & Conditions” page for full details or email us at info@twerkqueenlagos.com.
