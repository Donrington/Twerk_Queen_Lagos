FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with TwerkQueen Lagos?

+

To register, click on the "Register" button on the homepage. A form will appear where you can submit your details. Once submitted, the form will be sent directly to our admin for processing.

Is there an age restriction for participants?

+

Yes, participants must be 18 years or older to compete in the Twerk Queen Lagos competition. Please ensure you meet the age requirement before registering

What happens after I register?

+

Once you register, you'll be notified about the competition schedule. Your performance will be judged live at the event, and if you're selected, you can move to the Grand Finale where you can win N10 Million!

Do I need a partner?

+

Absolutely not! Twerking is about individual expression. Whether you come solo or with friends, TwerkQueen Lagos is open to all.

How do I submit my video?

+

You can submit your well-lit 1-minute twerking video by sending it to info.vhl@gmail.com. Be sure to follow the guidelines for better chances of getting voted in by your fans!

Can twerking help me reach fitness goals?

+

Definitely! Twerking engages core muscles, improves stamina, and offers a total-body cardio workout. You’ll see progress in no time.

What is your refund or cancellation policy?

+

We offer flexible class credits and partial refunds for monthly subscriptions. Check our “Terms & Conditions” page for full details or email us at info@twerkqueenlagos.com.
